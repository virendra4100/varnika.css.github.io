About Us

Varnika is a well known name in the world of handmade & casting jewelry manufacturer, located in Jaipur, the gem city of India, working in this field from past several years.Varnika is completely a personalized jewelry manufacturer and exporter which manufacture custom made jewelry pieces. Our company is manufacturing & exporting, Diamond jewelry, gold jewelry, fine silver jewelry, traditional jewelry, ethnic jewelry, fashion jewelry, costume jewelry with/without gemstones. We have master artisans who are well versed in making handmade and casting jewelry. Also we have highly skilled artisans who are specialized in making ribbon, thread, crochet, wire, leather and cord jewelry. We are very much committed to best quality at best prices & timely delivery.

Our jewelry range includes:
  • Rings
  • Earrings
  • Pendants
  • Necklaces
  • Bangles
  • Bracelets
  • Cufflinks
  • Brooches
  • Chains
  • Beads
